Privacy Policy

100% Local & Private Processing

Your privacy is our utmost priority. Unlike other online PDF tools that mandate uploading your highly sensitive documents to remote servers across the globe, PDFSuite is fundamentally architected to process everything locally inside your own web browser.

1. Data Collection & Files

We do not collect, store, view, or transmit your files. When you compress, merge, convert, split, or edit a PDF document using our platform, the heavy computational lifting happens entirely on your own device's CPU and memory via WebAssembly.

Because your private data never touches our servers, there is zero risk of data interception, hacking of server databases, or unauthorized viewing of your financial, legal, or personal documents by our team.

2. Third-Party Services

While the core PDF engine operates offline via your browser, our actual website uses a few standard services to keep the lights on:

  • Analytics: We may use basic, anonymized analytics to monitor website uptime, visitor counts, and page load speeds using standard technologies. This contains no document data.
  • Payments: All monetization for "PDFSuite PRO" happens through Razorpay's secure checkout gateway. We do not process, see, or store your credit card, bank, or UPI records on our end.
  • Advertisements: We utilize Google AdSense to serve ads to users to keep the basic toolset completely free. AdSense may use cookies to serve personalized advertisements based on your prior web browsing history.

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's internal "Local Storage" strictly to save your UI preferences (like remembering whether you prefer Dark or Light mode) and checking whether you have unlocked PRO features. Absolutely no personal information is kept there.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or inquiries regarding how your data is handled at PDFSuite, please use the Feedback button on our main homepage dashboard to get in touch with our team.
