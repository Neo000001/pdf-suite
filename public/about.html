About PDFSuite

"Democratizing Powerful Document Tools While Respecting Your Privacy"

PDFSuite was built as an independent, browser-first platform. Our vision is to offer the utility of expensive enterprise software without the privacy invasive practices of uploading sensitive medical, legal, and financial PDFs to random internet servers.

How It Works

Most online PDF tools take your file, upload it to an expensive cloud server, run traditional Linux processing tools, and make you download the result back. This is slow, expensive, and terrible for privacy.

Processing Right In Your Browser

By leveraging cutting-edge web technologies like WebAssembly, HTML5, and advanced JavaScript APIs, we compile powerful C/C++ PDF engines to run directly inside your Chrome or Safari browser. When you work with PDFSuite, you aren't sending files to the cloud—you're bringing the cloud directly to your local machine processing unit.

Unbreakable Privacy Standard

Because the network is entirely disconnected from your editing workflow, your files cannot be physically stolen by our database—because there is no database. Your medical forms, taxes, and work NDAs are incredibly safe here.

Lightning Fast Speeds

No waiting for a 100MB PDF to upload on a slow connection. No waiting in a "queue" behind other users. Processing starts instantly using your own CPU cores right on your desk.

Thank you for trusting and supporting our mission to keep the web open, secure, and incredibly fast.
